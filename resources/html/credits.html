Mapping
Mapping data courtesy of OpenStreetMap. Without OSM mappers on the ground collecting great data, CycleStreets would not be possible. OpenStreetMap data can be used freely under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 2.0 license.

OpenStreetMap is the Wiki of maps. YOU can help improve the routing and location data, by going to www.openstreetmap.org and contributing your local knowledge!

Routing by ourselves! - www.cyclestreets.net

 Feedback on planned routes is very welcome, at www.cyclestreets.net/feedback, and helps us improve the algorithms and data.

 Map tiles thanks to OpenStreetMap, and OpenCycleMap.
